Screen Actions

A fast, on-device way to turn on-screen text into Contacts, Calendar events, Reminders or a CSV.

Safari Web Extension & Share Extension

Select text on any webpage, tap the extension, and choose an action:

  • Add to Calendar — date/time detection, quick tweaks, save.
  • Create Reminder — due dates, lists and priority.
  • Extract Contact — name, phone, email and address into Contacts.
  • Receipt → CSV — pull line items and totals into a clean CSV.

Visual Scan

Scan a photo or screenshot. Text is processed by Apple’s on-device models (Vision/Data Detectors). No servers.

Shortcuts & App Intents

Trigger actions from Shortcuts or the Share sheet. Automate routine conversions without leaving the app you’re in.

No servers. No tracking. No ads.

Everything happens on your device. We don’t collect analytics or run any backend.

Made for iOS 26

Built with modern iOS frameworks. Supports app extensions and Live Activities where appropriate.
